BALANCE CALCULATION LOGIC:
- For each expense: payer is owed amountOwed by each split member
- Net balance per pair = total owed to you - total you owe them
- Simplify debts: greedy algorithm (sort creditors and debtors, match largest first)
- All amounts in paise (integer), display as ₹X.XX using currency_formatter.dart
